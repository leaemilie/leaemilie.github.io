Léa's homemade
Tarte Tatin recipe

For 6-8 people

Want to impress your friends?
Learn how to make this delicious french apple pie recipe to wow your guests!
No experience necessary.

Utensils

  • a measuring glass
  • a big bowl to make the dough
  • a bowl to contain the diced apples
  • a rolling pin
  • a strainer
  • 1 glass for the water or milk
  • 1 big spoon, 1 little spoon
  • 1 fork
  • parchment paper
  • a small or medium pan to make the caramel
  • a pie pan

Ingredients

    For the Dough

  • 300g of all purpose flour
  • 150g of butter
  • half a teaspoon of salt
  • 3 big spoons of sugar
  • 8cl of water or milk
  • cinnamon powder
  • nutmeg powder
  • vanilla extract
    For the Apple mix

  • 100g of sugar
  • 50g of butter
  • 2 honey crisp apples
  • lime zest
  • cinnamon powder
  • nutmeg powder
  • vanilla extract

See quantities in cups

Preheat the Oven

at 410°F or 210°C.

Making the Dough

  1. Strain the flour in a bowl and mix it with the salt and sugar.
  2. Add the cinnamon and nutmeg powders, with a few drops of the vanilla extract.
  3. Add the butter to the mix and start kneading it into a dough.
  4. Add the water or milk until reaching the desired dough consistency.
  5. Flatten out the dough on parchment paper and roll it out in a circle shape.
  6. Poke the dough repeatedly with a fork to prevent it from rising and exploding in the oven.

Pro tip: the faster the process, the better the dough consistency.

Making the Caramel

  1. Place the butter and sugar in the pan on low heat.
  2. Let cook for 5 min. The caramel will form itself.

Preparing the Apples

Cut the apples in big cubes and place them in a bowl.

Pro tip: if you want a finish like on the picture at the beginning, add a 3rd apple and cut all of them in quarters.

Assembling the Pie

  1. Cover the entire surface of the bottom of the pie pan with the caramel.
  2. Place the apples on the caramel and fill the pie pan with them.
  3. Sprinkle the apples with cinnamon and nutmeg powder.
  4. Add lime zest and vanilla extract on the apples.
  5. Cover the apples with the dough. Remove the parchment paper carefully.
  6. Place the pie in the oven and bake for at least 25 minutes.
  7. When the crust is browning, the pie is ready.

Serve

Upside down on a big serving plate and accompany your slice with vanilla ice cream.
